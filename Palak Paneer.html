Palak Paneer (Spinach & Cottage Cheese Curry)

A creamy spinach-based curry with soft paneer cubes

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cut into cubes)
  • 2 cups Spinach leaves (blanched and pureed)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam masala
  • 2 tbsp Cream
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Mix in tomato puree, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add the spinach puree and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add salt and garam masala. Stir well.
  6. Add paneer cubes and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Drizzle cream on top, mix gently, and serve hot with naan or roti.
