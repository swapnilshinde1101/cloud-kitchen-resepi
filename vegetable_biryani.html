Vegetable Biryani

Aromatic rice and veggies in a perfect blend of spices

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 1 cup Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and set it aside.
  2. In a pan, sauté onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add mixed vegetables, yogurt, and biryani masala. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Layer the cooked rice over the vegetables and cook on low heat for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with raita or salad.
