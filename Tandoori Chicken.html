Tandoori Chicken

Deliciously marinated chicken cooked to perfection with smoky flavors

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 1 tsp Cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, cumin powder, oil, and salt to make a marinade.
  2. Coat the chicken pieces with the marinade and let them marinate for at least 2-3 hours or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F). Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray lined with parchment paper or a grill rack.
  4. Bake the chicken for 25-30 minutes, turning halfway through, until the chicken is cooked and slightly charred on the outside.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
