Paneer Butter Masala

Creamy and delicious paneer in a rich buttery tomato gravy

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1/4 cup Cream
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Sauté onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Mix in garam masala and cream, then gently add paneer cubes.
  5. Simmer for 5-7 minutes and serve hot with naan or rice.
