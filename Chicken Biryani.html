Chicken Biryani

Aromatic and flavorful rice cooked with spiced chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken (bone-in or boneless)
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp Biryani masala
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • 4-5 Cloves
  • 3-4 Cardamom pods
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 1 small Cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes. Cook until 80% done and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a large pan. Add cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, and bay leaf. Sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Mix in chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and biryani masala. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add chicken and yogurt. Cook on medium heat until the chicken is tender and the gravy thickens.
  6. Layer the cooked rice over the chicken mixture. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves.
  7. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat (dum) for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita or salad.
