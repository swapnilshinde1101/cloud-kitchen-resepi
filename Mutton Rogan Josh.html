Mutton Rogan Josh

A rich and flavorful curry made with tender mutton, yogurt, and aromatic spices

Ingredients

  • 500g Mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 2 Onions (finely sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 2-3 dried Kashmiri red chilies
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds

Steps

  1. Heat oil and ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, cinnamon stick, cloves, and dried red chilies. Let them splutter.
  2. Fry sliced onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add the mutton pieces and sear them on high heat for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Reduce heat, add yogurt, and cook the mutton until it is tender (about 40-45 minutes).
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or steamed rice.
