Fish Curry

A delicious and tangy curry made with fresh fish and aromatic spices

Ingredients

  • 500g Fish (any firm variety, cleaned and cut)
  • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 cup Coconut milk
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp Fenugreek seeds
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh curry leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Allow them to splutter, then add fenugreek seeds and curry leaves.
  2. Sauté onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  3. Mix in tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add coconut milk and bring the mixture to a gentle boil.
  5. Add the fish pieces and simmer on low heat until the fish is cooked through (about 10-12 minutes).
  6. Adjust salt and garnish with fresh curry leaves before serving.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
