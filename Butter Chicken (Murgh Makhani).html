Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

A rich and creamy tomato-based chicken curry

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • 2 tbsp Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
  • 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 1/2 cup Fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until slightly browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté onions until golden brown, then add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Add garam masala, kasuri methi, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan and simmer in the sauce for 10 minutes.
  6. Stir in fresh cream and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
