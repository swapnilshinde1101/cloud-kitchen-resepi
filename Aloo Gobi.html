Aloo Gobi (Potato & Cauliflower Curry)

A classic North Indian curry made with potatoes, cauliflower, and aromatic spices

Ingredients

  • 2 Potatoes (diced)
  • 1 small Cauliflower (cut into florets)
  • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Allow them to splutter.
  2. Sauté onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Mix in tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add the diced potatoes and cauliflower florets. Stir to coat with the spices.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
