Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Delicious and spicy chickpea curry packed with flavor

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp Chole masala
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas with a pinch of salt until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and sauté onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and chole masala. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Mix in the boiled chickpeas and cook for 10–15 minutes, adjusting the gravy consistency with water.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
